StadiumVIP - Luxury & Excellence

At the SPCA National Stadium, we provide facilities and service that is only the best for our customers. This is why we have launched StadiumVIP, our extremely exclusive VIP lounge. At StadiumVIP, we offer member-only exclusives at our state-of-the-art StadiumVIP facilities. These include VIP bars, lounges, member-only discounts on events, special parking, VIP stadium seats, and much, much more!


A StadumVIP membership comes in five different tiers. These tiers allow you to possess big rewards and benefits that only StadiumVIP members can access. Tiers are listed on the top right with their benefits.


Frequently Asked Questions:


Q: How can I get a StadiumVIP membership?

A: It is very easy to apply for a StadiumVIP membership. Simply enquire below, give us a call through the number listed on the Contact Us page, or even come visit us at the reception of our Executive Lounge


Q: How much does it cost to hold a StadiumVIP membership?

A: We offer different prices for our five tiers. They go as follows:

Midnight Blue: $40 per month + GST

Ruby Steel: $60 per month + GST

Jade Green: $75 per month + GST

Rose Gold: $100 per month + GST

Obsidian: $150 per month + GST


Q: What is the difference between the Lounge and the Executive Lounge?

A: The key difference is the products offered as part of the lounge. For example, foods and beverages will be of greater rarity and exclusivity at the Executive Lounge. Moreover, the Executive Lounge provides a personal one-to-one experience with your time tailored exclusively for you.
