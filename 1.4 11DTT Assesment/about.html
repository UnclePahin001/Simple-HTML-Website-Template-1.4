About Us

We are Aotearoa New Zealand's first Carbon Zero Stadium

At our heart is a superb performance space, attracting the biggest names in the world of sports & entertainment since 2007. Along with large-scale concerts and events, SPCA National Stadium's strength lies in its ability to be configured into intimate modes, scaling exactly to accommodate audiences from 1,800 to 12,000 in the main arena, and 100-300 in the StadiumVIP lounge space. With a convenient central location, SPCA National Stadium is within walking distance from the Auckland City Centre and the major public transport hubs: Britomart railway station & bus terminal, and the downtown ferry terminal. More than just a building for hire, SPCA National Stadium's in-house management team has a long track record in international events management and is proactive in helping each event fulfill its unique potential.
